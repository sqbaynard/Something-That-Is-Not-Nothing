What the Words Held

A word given. A scenario returned. Not an explanation — a shape. What arrived before thinking could interfere.

Every scenario kept its reason.
The reason was mine to hold.

What remained was the shape —
the thing the word became
when I reached toward it honestly.

A room that remembers music.
A door left open.
Still there. After everything. Still there.
